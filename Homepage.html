What Do We Do?

Guidance. Assistance. Assurance.

Three things of utmost importance that people fighting cancer require the most. We help those souls in need, guiding them to the best possible treatment centers for them and assisting them at each step, along the way. You may rest assured that with us, you will be recommended the best possible facilities for you, which will provide you with all the help you need, health-related, financially or otherwise.

You aren’t alone in this battle.

Our Work

We analyse your requirements of treatment, lodging or financial aid, and accordingly connect you to NGOs which are best suited to assist you, to banks which are the most accommodating to sponsor your treatment and to HOA’s and other centers which will arrange for your stay for the duration of your treatment. With us, you will find all the help you need at one place.

  • Over the last decade, we have helped out over a million fighters overcome this battle.
  • We have business affiliations with the top 10 NGO’s in Mumbai for cancer treatment.
  • Over 5 lakh HOA members and boarding administrators are registered with us.
  • Every year, we cater to over 50,000+ fighters, with nobody till date going away empty-handed.
