Help Desk works in 18 Indian states and more than 80 countries around the world. Globally, it has been in operation since 2001 and has impacted the lives of more than 10 million people in India since 2008.




Face to Face fundraising is one of the key fundraising initiatives for Help Desk. Face to Face fundraisers are 'our Face' on the street. We sincerely thank and are immensely proud of our fundraisers who engage in Door-to-door, street/outdoor fundraising and other forms of Face to Face fundraising. They bring in a significant amount of donations for us, which are then utilized for the work Save the Children does, which included running programmes on Health & Nutrition, Child Protection, Education and Humanitarian work.
